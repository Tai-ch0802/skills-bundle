## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please follow these steps:

1.  **Do not open a public issue.** Security vulnerabilities should be handled discreetly to protect users.
2.  **Email the maintainers.** Please send an email to [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS] with details about the vulnerability.
3.  **Includes details.** Provide as much information as possible, including steps to reproduce the issue, the affected version(s), and potential impact.

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and work with you to verify and resolve the issue. We appreciate your responsible disclosure and efforts to make this project more secure.
